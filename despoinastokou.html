Despina Stokou (born 1978 in Athens, Greece) is a contemporary artist, writer and curator based in Los Angeles, California and she studied at the Athens School of Fine Arts and at the University of Arts in Berlin. She primarily produces gestural, expressive paintings, often large and displaying vivid color, that include layered collage elements like cut paper letters spelling out pointed phrases and topical passages that tumble and pile up across her canvases. Idealism, feminism, and a strong use of painterly tradition characterize her work. Stokou makes paintings that explore popular culture and political debates as mediated by contemporary public forums. She does so, by creating artworks that are created by monochrome slashes and drips scuffling with waves of smeared or overlapping text.
